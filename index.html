Homemade Bubbles

With the height of the corona virus about, my children quickly took to blowing bubbles during March afternoons. After being ordered to stay home and homeschool, it was a light activity to keep them entertained and outside catching some Vitamin D.

Photo Credits: Home Science Tools: How to Make Bubbles

    Ingredients:

  • Liquid Dish Soap
  • Water
  • Container with Lid
  • Sugar, Glycerin or Light Corn Syrup
  • Bubble Wand or Straw

    Steps:

  1. Measure 6 cups of water into one container
  2. Pour 1 cup of dish soap into the water and slowly stir it until the soap is mixed in.
  3. Try not to let foam or bubbles form while you stir.
  4. Measure 1 tablespoon of glycerin or 1/4 cup of corn syrup and add it to the container.
  5. Stir the solution until it is mixed together.
